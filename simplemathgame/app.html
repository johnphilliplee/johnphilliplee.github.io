About the App

Simple Math Game transforms quick calculations into an exciting challenge. Test your skills across multiple game modes, race against the clock, and sharpen your mental agility. Whether you’re warming up your brain or competing for the fastest score, every round keeps you on your toes.

Features

  • Multiple quiz modes for variety and replayability
  • Time‑based challenges to push your speed and accuracy
  • Clean, straightforward design for focused gameplay
  • Stats and history
